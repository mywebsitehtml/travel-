Featured Destinations

Coonoor, Tamil Nadu

A quieter alternative to Ooty, Coonoor is surrounded by tea gardens and offers beautiful views, perfect for a peaceful getaway.

Araku Valley, Andhra Pradesh

Nestled in the Eastern Ghats, Araku Valley is famous for its lush green landscapes, coffee plantations, and a tranquil atmosphere, making it a popular getaway.

Vanzangi, Andhra Pradesh

Vanzangi is a hidden gem in the Eastern Ghats, known for its scenic beauty, lush greenery, and tranquility, making it an ideal spot for nature lovers.

Ooty, Tamil Nadu

Known as the "Queen of Hill Stations," Ooty features beautiful gardens, lakes, and a pleasant climate, making it a popular yet serene destination.
